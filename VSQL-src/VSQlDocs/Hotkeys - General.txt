General Hotkeys -- May '05

The items in the "Tools" menu can also be accessed directly from
either the map or controls window, with these commands:

      Roll 1 Die              -- CTRL-1 or F1
      Roll 2 Dice             -- CTRL-2 or F2
      Zoom map out            -- CTRL-3 or F3
      Zoom map in             -- CTRL-4 or F4
      Charts and Tables       -- CTRL-5 or F5
      Internet Dice Roller    -- CTRL-6 or F6
      Scenario Info Window    -- CTRL-7 or F7
      Check LOS               -- CTRL-0 or F10
      Page Down               -- Step through open log file
      Right-Click any counter -- Popup menu of Counter options
      Right-Click on map      -- Scrolls map in that direction
      Click and Drag on map   -- Draws a rectangle to select multiple counters

You may chat with the mouse in any of the VASL windows, instead of
entering text into the control window.

You may scroll the map with the arrow keys if no counters are selected
(click on empty hex to de-select counters).
